We provide design, manufacturing, prototyping and feasibility consultation for a wide range of product development work, all under one roof. Our team’s depth and breadth of experience means we can navigate product development challenges, design to your strengths and save you time and money. We believe changes made in-house at the top level result in the best value to our clients.

Our Efficient Design Process

  • Eliminates overhead costs associated with engaging & coordinating multiple engineering and manufacturing firms
  • Leverages relationships with subcontractors
  • Integrates materials, means, and methods knowledge gained through our interdisciplinary business model.
  • Allows easy and fluid real-time changes to designs, e.g., we can walk out the door of the design studio and into the shop to interface with the projects in process.
  • Anticipates business / planning consequences & benefits in design stages pertaining to branding, marketing, manufacturing, and fulfillment issues.
  • Maintains a culture of good stewardship of our clients’ Intellectual Property and the manufacturing standards of their products in-house & out-sourced.
  • Reduces costly production problems that typically happen as a result of poor communication and maintenance of documentation.
  • Results in reduction of time loss due to coordination of multiple contractors
